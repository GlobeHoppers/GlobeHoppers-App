# GlobeHoppers v7.5.15 Screensaver Presentation

Screensaver Mode remains available through `?screensaver=1`, but now uses a dedicated presentation surface.

## Visible elements

- Animated globe, routes, locations, and active vessel
- Current Hop card and queued Hop cards
- Full-width bottom timeline, including pins, progress, date labels, and active-Hop callout

## Hidden elements

- GlobeHoppers title, tagline, and About copy
- Add Hop, Hoppers, GlobeHopper Timeline, Globe View, Fullscreen, and playback buttons
- Timeline zoom, Fit, Recenter, Search, and advanced controls
- Globe spin controls, map zoom diagnostics, and authoring/editor surfaces

The timeline-to-Globe-View-to-timeline cycle remains unchanged. Standard non-screensaver URLs continue to show the complete interactive interface.
