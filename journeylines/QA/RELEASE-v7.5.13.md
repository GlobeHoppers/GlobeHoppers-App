# GlobeHoppers v7.5.13 Release Notes

- Active timeline pins begin in their fully raised state immediately after selection.
- Active-pin stems remain extended and callout pills remain attached above the pin head.
- Short Choose-a-Hop lists use compact, top-aligned cards rather than vertically stretched cards.
- The top-menu Globe button is larger and easier to identify and select.
- Existing v7.5.12 transport badges and globe-mode playback isolation are preserved.
