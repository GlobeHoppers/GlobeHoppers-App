# GlobeHoppers v7.5.12 Interaction Notes

## Search and destination results
- Search and Choose-a-Hop cards show the distinct transportation modes used by the Hop's legs.
- Plane, Car, Train, and Boat are presented as compact badges with matching icons.
- Mixed-mode Hops retain first-use order while repeated modes are shown once, keeping the card compact.
- Transportation modes participate in Timeline search matching.

## Active timeline pin
- The active pin stem is twice its previous length.
- The active pin head rises by the added stem length, so the stem remains attached to the same timeline position.
- The active Hop pill follows the raised pin head and remains attached above it.
- Hover pins retain their existing geometry.

## Globe display filters
- Routes only and Locations only are scoped exclusively to explicit Globe View.
- Starting, resuming, jumping to, or relocating during playback presents both routes and locations.
- Hero and idle presentation also show both routes and locations.
