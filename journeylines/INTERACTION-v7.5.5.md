# GlobeHoppers v7.5.5 Interaction Stabilization

## Camera
Follow mode now uses one stable zoom baseline per leg. Endpoint and arrival treatment may zoom inward, but normal playback does not reduce zoom after route acquisition. Manual camera reacquisition remains staged: safe orientation first, then final route zoom.

## Timeline
Year labels remain horizontal and unmasked. Month labels are created only for months containing a Hop and are positioned at that Hop's timeline pin. The rail height is fixed and active-pin callouts may render beyond the rail.

## Result cards
Search and destination-selection surfaces share the same card geometry: approximately 75% for title and Hopper identity and 25% for route and date. Hopper names use their assigned colors.

## Globe menu
The first menu entry is Globe View, followed by Routes only and Locations only.
