# GlobeHoppers v7.5.13 Interaction Update

## Active timeline marker

The active marker is now positioned from the fixed timeline-track baseline. Its stem and head are rendered as independent overlay elements, so a newly selected Hop immediately appears at the same fully raised position previously reached only after hover. The active stem remains twice the normal length and the callout stays attached above the raised head.

## Destination result density

Choose-a-Hop result grids align their rows to the top of the available panel. Small result sets retain the same compact minimum card height and spacing as long scrollable result sets instead of stretching to fill the full vertical rail.

## Globe action

The top-menu Globe button has a larger dedicated hit target and icon while adjacent Fullscreen and Playback controls retain their existing dimensions.
